Tiramisu

Description

This Italian tiramisù recipe is easy to whip up, this version is made with cooked (instead of raw) eggs, and it tastes just as indulgent and delicious as ever.

Who does not love an easy no-bake dessert? That is one of the best things about this tiramisu recipe.

Ingredients

Ladyfingers

Mascarpone: it wouldn’t be true tiramisu without mascarpone, but if you absolutely must, you could substitute cream cheese.

Coffee: I use espresso

Heavy Whipped cream

Granulated Sugar

Vanilla extract: or substitute imitation vanilla

Cocoa powder: for dusting on top

Method

  1. Mix creamy filling: Beat the mascarpone, cream, sugar, and vanilla together until stiff peaks.
  2. Dip lady fingers:Add the espresso and liqueur (if using) to a shallow bowl and dip the lady fingers on both sides (don’t let them soak–just a quick dip!)
  3. Layer mascarpone:Smooth a layer of the mascarpone/whipped cream mixture on top of the lady fingers.
  4. Repeat:Add another layer of lady fingers (dipped in coffee and liqueur) and another layer of cheese mixture. Dust with cocoa powder.
