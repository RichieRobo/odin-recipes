Chicken Teriyaki Don

Description

This can be a super quick & easy meal that everyone would be satisfied with.

For this rice bowl dish, add plenty of grated fresh Ginger.

And of course you cannot forget green vegetables.

Ingredients

300g Chicken Thigh Fillets

Salt

Oil

4 Spring Onions cut to 4-5cm

Green vegetables such as chinese broccoli,

green beans or fresh cucmber

2 servings Freshly Cooked Rice

Toasted Sesame Seeds

Teriyaki Sauce

1 small piece Ginger grated

1 tablespoon Sugar

2 tablespoons Soy Sauce

1 tablespoon Mirin

Method

  1. Combine the Ginger Teriyaki Sauce ingredients in a small bowl.
  2. Lightly season Chicken Fillets with Salt. Heat a small amount of Oil in a frying pan (*OR no Oil if non-stick pan is used) over medium-high heat, cook Chicken Fillets and Vegetables. If vegetables are cooked before Chicken is cooked, just transfer them to a plate. When Chicken Fillets are browned on both sides, pour the sauce over and keep cooking until the sauce thickens. Return the vegetables and combine with the sauce.
  3. Half fill a bowl with Freshly Cooked Rice, place cut Chicken Fillets and vegetables on top, drizzle the sauce over and sprinkle with Toasted Sesame Seeds.
